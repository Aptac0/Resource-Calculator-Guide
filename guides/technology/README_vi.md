# Huong Dan Cong Nghe

**[Español](README_es.md) | [English](README_en.md) | [Português](README_pt.md) | Tiếng Việt | [Bahasa Indonesia](README_id.md) | [Français](README_fr.md)**

Huong dan nhanh de uu tien nghien cuu cho tai khoan farm va ho tro.

## Uu tien kinh te

1. Toc do thu thap
2. San luong tai nguyen
3. Suc chua van chuyen
4. Toc do xay dung
5. Toc do nghien cuu

## Uu tien quan su (toi thieu)

- Chi nang cap nhung muc bat buoc cho yeu cau cong trinh va tien do.
- Tranh rut tai nguyen khoi nhanh kinh te tren tai khoan farm.

## Khuyen nghi thuc te

- Duy tri nghien cuu lien tuc 24/7.
- Dung tang toc cho nghien cuu dai.
- Dong bo nghien cuu voi muc tieu Toa Thi Chinh.
