Quick guide to prioritize research for farm and support accounts.

## Economic priority

1. Gathering speed
2. Resource production
3. Load capacity
4. Building speed
5. Research speed

## Military priority (minimal)

- Upgrade only what is required for building requirements and progression.
- Avoid shifting resources away from the economic branch on farm accounts.

## Practical recommendation

- Keep research running 24/7.
- Use boosts on long researches.
- Align research with your City Hall goals.
