# Gathering Commanders Guide

**[Español](README_es.md) | English | [Português](README_pt.md) | [Tiếng Việt](README_vi.md) | [Bahasa Indonesia](README_id.md) | [Français](README_fr.md)**

Quick guide to level gathering commanders efficiently.

## Recommended order

1. Sarka
2. Gaius Marius
3. Centurion
4. Joan of Arc
5. Matilda / Cleopatra / Ishida / Seondeok

## Suggested progression

1. Level Sarka and Gaius to 20.
2. Level Centurion to 20.
3. At City Hall 17, level Joan to 20.
4. Level all 5 gatherers to 27.

## Capacity goals

- Mid goal: 630000 per march.
- Final goal: 1260000 per march.

## Key recommendations

- Use gatherers as primary commanders.
- Prioritize gathering talents before combat talents.
- Train troops to increase load capacity.
