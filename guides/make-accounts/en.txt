Start – Level 1 to 3

Upgrade the City Hall to Level 3 first (≈5 min).

While it's upgrading: upgrade the Lumber Mill, Farm, Barracks, and Archery Range as much as possible.

Once it's finished, upgrade the Wall to Level 2 and the Tavern to Level 2.

Use Speedups on the Tavern if necessary.

─────────────────────────────

City Hall 3 → 4

Upgrade the City Hall to Level 4 (≈20 min).

While it's upgrading: continue upgrading the Farm, Lumber Mill, Barracks, and Hospital.

Make sure the Wall reaches Level 3.

─────────────────────────────

City Hall 4 → 5

Upgrade the Hospital to Level 4★ and the Wall to Level 4★.

At the same time, upgrade all Farms and Lumber Mills to Level 4, improve the Scout Camp, and train basic troops.

Then upgrade the City Hall to Level 5 (≈45 min with Alliance Help).

─────────────────────────────

City Hall 5 → 6

Requirements: Quarry 5★, Tavern 5★, Wall 5★, Scout Camp 5★.

While upgrading the Quarry and Tavern, queue upgrades for the Farm and Lumber Mill.

Use Speedups on the Quarry and Tavern, then upgrade the City Hall to Level 6.

BUILDING TIPS

Keep two Builders active (VIP or Gem Builder).

Use Speedups only on the City Hall and ★ required buildings.

Request Alliance Help before every long upgrade.

Keep gathering Stone and Wood continuously; they are the main bottleneck.

Research Construction and Economy technologies in the Academy from the very beginning.

─────────────────────────────
Once you reach City Hall Level 17, your main objective is to maximize your gathering efficiency as quickly as possible.

First, upgrade **only one** Farm, Lumber Mill, Quarry, and Goldmine to Level 17. There is no need to upgrade the remaining resource buildings yet.

After those four buildings reach Level 17, upgrade your training buildings (Barracks, Archery Range, and Stable) to Level 17 as well. The Siege Workshop can remain at Level 10, since it is not required for this strategy.

Next, upgrade the Alliance Center, Academy, Tavern, Wall, Storehouse, and Hospital to Level 17.

If your resource sales are still low or you do not need to produce large amounts of resources yet, the best option is to continue directly toward City Hall Level 22. Reaching this level unlocks a fifth march, significantly increasing your gathering capacity. It also prevents you from spending unnecessary resources on buildings that provide little benefit for a gathering-focused farm account.

If needed in the future, you can always continue developing these accounts. However, for a farm account, City Hall 22 offers an excellent balance between investment, time, and efficiency.
