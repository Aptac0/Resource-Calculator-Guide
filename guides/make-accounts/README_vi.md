# Huong Dan Tao Tai Khoan Farm

**[Español](README_es.md) | [English](README_en.md) | [Português](README_pt.md) | Tiếng Việt | [Bahasa Indonesia](README_id.md) | [Français](README_fr.md)**

Lo trinh de xuat de nang tai khoan farm tu Toa Thi Chinh 1 len 22 mot cach nhanh va hieu qua.

## Muc tieu

- Dat Toa Thi Chinh 22 nhanh de mo dao quan thu 5.
- Uu tien cong trinh bat buoc va tranh ton tai nguyen khong can thiet.
- Xay dung tai khoan co kha nang tao tai nguyen on dinh cho tai khoan chinh.

## Cac giai doan tien trinh chinh

1. Toa Thi Chinh 1 den 17: hoan thanh dieu kien toi thieu moi cap.
2. Tai cap 17: nang 1 Nong Trai, 1 Xuong Go, 1 Mo Da va 1 Mo Vang len cap 17.
3. Nang cac cong trinh huan luyen va ho tro thiet yeu len cap 17.
4. Tiep tuc len Toa Thi Chinh 22 chi bang cac dieu kien bat buoc.

## Tap trung vao tung giai doan

### Giai doan 1 den 11

- Khong phan tan tai nguyen vao cong trinh khong bat buoc.
- Day nhanh Toa Thi Chinh va de danh tang toc cho giai doan dai hon.

### Giai doan 12 den 17

- Giu nhip dieu kien nang cap ma khong de builder dung.
- Uu tien da va go de tranh ket chuoi nang cap quan trong.

### Giai doan 17 den 22

- Day thang len 22 voi cach tiep can toi thieu bat buoc.
- Tranh nang cap le khong giup mo dao quan thu 5.

## Uu tien

- Duy tri 2 builder hoat dong.
- Dung tang toc cho Toa Thi Chinh va cong trinh bat buoc.
- Xin tro giup lien minh truoc cac nang cap dai.
- Uu tien thu thap da va go.

## Quan ly tang toc va tai nguyen

- De danh tang toc cho cong trinh chinh va cac diem nghen.
- Tranh dung tang toc cho nang cap ngan neu khong mo khoa dieu kien.
- Luon kiem tra dieu kien tiep theo truoc khi bat dau nang cap dai.

## Loi thuong gap

- Nang cap cong trinh phu qua som.
- De mot builder khong hoat dong do lap ke hoach kem.
- Tieu tai nguyen vao quan khong can thiet truoc khi dam bao tien do Toa Thi Chinh.

## Checklist nhanh truoc khi nang Toa Thi Chinh

1. Da co du dieu kien chinh xac cho cap tiep theo chua?
2. Da co du da va go de tiep tuc lien tuc chua?
3. Da xin tro giup lien minh de giam thoi gian chua?
4. Da xep hang mot nang cap huu ich trong khi nang cap chinh chua?

## Ket qua mong doi

- Mo dao quan thu 5 tai Toa Thi Chinh 22.
- Tang hieu qua thu thap cho tai khoan farm.
- Nen tang on dinh de tao tai nguyen theo chu ky farm dai.

## Meo nhip do (giai doan 1 den 17)

- Luon giu 2 builder hoat dong (VIP hoac gem).
- Chi dung tang toc cho Toa Thi Chinh va cong trinh co dau ★.
- Xin tro giup lien minh truoc moi nang cap dai.

- Nghien cuu Cong Trinh va Kinh Te tai Hoc Vien ngay tu dau.

## Chuyen doi chien luoc tai cap 17

Sau khi dat cap 17, muc tieu la tang hieu qua thu thap nhanh nhat co the.

1. Chi nang mot Nong Trai, mot Xuong Go, mot Mo Da va mot Mo Vang len cap 17.
2. Sau do nang cong trinh huan luyen (Doanh Trai Bo Binh, Trai Cung Thu, Chuong Ky Binh) len cap 17.
3. Xuong Cong Thanh co the giu toi da cap 10 cho muc tieu nay.
4. Tiep theo nang Trung Tam Lien Minh, Hoc Vien, Quan Ruou, Tuong, Kho va Benh Vien len cap 17.

Neu viec ban tai nguyen van thap hoac ban chua can san luong lon, cach hieu qua nhat la day thang len Toa Thi Chinh 22. O cap nay ban mo dao quan thu 5 va tang manh suc chua thu thap ma khong lang phi vao nang cap phu.

Voi tai khoan farm, Toa Thi Chinh 22 la diem can bang rat tot giua dau tu, thoi gian va hieu qua.
