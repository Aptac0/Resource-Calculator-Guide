# Huong Dan Tao Tai Khoan Farm

**[Español](README_es.md) | [English](README_en.md) | [Português](README_pt.md) | Tiếng Việt | [Bahasa Indonesia](README_id.md) | [Français](README_fr.md)**

Lo trinh de xuat de nang tai khoan farm tu Toa Thi Chinh 1 len 22.

## Muc tieu

- Dat Toa Thi Chinh 22 nhanh de mo dao quan thu 5.
- Uu tien cong trinh bat buoc va tranh ton tai nguyen khong can thiet.

## Cac giai doan chinh

1. Toa Thi Chinh 1 den 17: hoan thanh dieu kien toi thieu moi cap.
2. Tai cap 17: chi nang 1 Nong Trai, 1 Xuong Go, 1 Mo Da, 1 Mo Vang len 17.
3. Nang cac cong trinh huan luyen va ho tro can thiet len 17.
4. Tiep tuc len Toa Thi Chinh 22 chi voi dieu kien bat buoc.

## Uu tien

- Luon giu 2 builder hoat dong.
- Dung tang toc cho Toa Thi Chinh va cong trinh bat buoc.
- Xin tro giup lien minh truoc nang cap dai.
- Uu tien thu thap da va go.

## Ket qua mong doi

- Mo dao quan thu 5 o Toa Thi Chinh 22.
- Tang hieu qua thu thap cho tai khoan farm.
