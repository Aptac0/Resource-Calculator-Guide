# Farm Account Creation Guide

**[Español](README_es.md) | English | [Português](README_pt.md) | [Tiếng Việt](README_vi.md) | [Bahasa Indonesia](README_id.md) | [Français](README_fr.md)**

Recommended route to move farm accounts from City Hall 1 to 22 quickly and efficiently.

## Goal

- Reach City Hall 22 quickly to unlock the fifth march.
- Prioritize mandatory buildings and avoid unnecessary spending.
- Build an account that produces resources consistently for your main account.

## Key progression phases

1. City Hall 1 to 17: complete minimum requirements for each level.
2. At 17: upgrade 1 Farm, 1 Lumber Mill, 1 Quarry, and 1 Goldmine to level 17.
3. Upgrade essential training and support buildings to level 17.
4. Continue to City Hall 22 with requirement-only progression.

## What to focus on at each stage

### Stage 1 to 11

- Do not spread resources into non-mandatory buildings.
- Push City Hall quickly and save speedups for longer stages.

### Stage 12 to 17

- Keep requirement pace without pausing builders.
- Prioritize stone and wood to avoid bottlenecks.

### Stage 17 to 22

- Push directly to 22 with a strict minimum-requirement focus.
- Avoid side upgrades that do not help unlock the fifth march.

## Priorities

- Keep 2 builders active.
- Use speedups on City Hall and required buildings.
- Ask alliance help before long upgrades.
- Prioritize stone and wood gathering.

## Speedup and resource management

- Save speedups for the main building and bottlenecks.
- Avoid using speedups on short upgrades that do not unlock requirements.
- Always check the next requirement before starting a long upgrade.

## Common mistakes

- Upgrading secondary buildings too early.
- Leaving one builder idle due to poor planning.
- Spending resources on unnecessary troops before securing City Hall progress.

## Quick checklist before upgrading City Hall

1. Do I have all exact requirements for the next level?
2. Do I have enough stone and wood to continue without stopping?
3. Did I request alliance help to reduce time?
4. Do I have a useful upgrade queued while the main one runs?

## Expected outcome

- Fifth march unlocked at City Hall 22.
- Better gathering efficiency for farm accounts.
- A stable base to produce resources in long farming cycles.

## Full step-by-step guide (City Hall 1 to 22)

### Start: level 1 to 3

- Start by upgrading City Hall to level 3 (about 5 min).
- While it runs: upgrade Lumber Mill, Farm, Infantry Barracks, and Archery Range as much as possible.
- When finished, upgrade Wall to 2 and Tavern to 2.
- Speed up Tavern if needed.

### City Hall 3 to 4

- Upgrade City Hall to 4 (about 20 min).
- While waiting: keep upgrading Farm, Lumber Mill, Barracks, and Hospital.
- Make sure Wall reaches level 3.

### City Hall 4 to 5

- Upgrade Hospital to 4★ and Wall to 4★.
- In parallel: upgrade all farms and lumber mills to 4, improve Scout Camp, and train basic troops.
- Then upgrade City Hall to 5 (about 45 min with help).

### City Hall 5 to 6

Requirements: Quarry 5★, Tavern 5★, Wall 5★, Scout Camp 5★.

- While upgrading Quarry and Tavern, queue Farm and Lumber Mill.
- Use speedups on Quarry and Tavern.
- Upgrade City Hall to 6.

### City Hall 6 to 7

Requirements: Quarry 6★, Tavern 6★, Wall 6★, Storehouse 6★.

- In parallel: Hospital and troops.
- Speed up Storehouse.
- Upgrade City Hall to 7.

### City Hall 7 to 8

Requirements: Quarry 7★, Tavern 7★, Wall 7★, Farm 7★, Barracks 7★.

- While upgrading Quarry and Farm, upgrade all resource buildings.
- Upgrade City Hall to 8.

### City Hall 8 to 9

Requirements: Quarry 8★, Tavern 8★, Wall 8★, Alliance Center 8★.

- Upgrade Hospital and Academy while waiting.
- Speed up Alliance Center.
- Upgrade City Hall to 9.

### City Hall 9 to 10

Requirements: Quarry 9★, Tavern 9★, Wall 9★, Academy 9★.

- Upgrade Hospitals and troops in the meantime.
- Upgrade City Hall to 10.

### City Hall 10 to 11

Requirements: Quarry 10★, Tavern 10★, Wall 10★, Stable 10★, Hospital 10★.

- Speed up Stable and Hospital.
- Upgrade City Hall to 11.

### City Hall 11 to 12

Requirements: Quarry 11★, Tavern 11★, Wall 11★, Storehouse 11★.

- Upgrade resources and troops.
- Upgrade City Hall to 12.

### City Hall 12 to 13

Requirements: Quarry 12★, Tavern 12★, Wall 12★, Lumber Mill 12★, Archery Range 12★.

- While waiting, upgrade Hospitals and troops.
- Upgrade City Hall to 13.

### City Hall 13 to 14

Requirements: Quarry 13★, Tavern 13★, Wall 13★, Alliance Center 13★, Goldmine 13★, Trading Post 13★.

- Speed up Goldmine and Trading Post.
- Upgrade City Hall to 14.

### City Hall 14 to 15

Requirements: Quarry 14★, Tavern 14★, Wall 14★, Scout Camp 14★.

- In parallel: Hospital and Academy.
- Upgrade City Hall to 15.

### City Hall 15 to 16

Requirements: Quarry 15★, Tavern 15★, Wall 15★, Academy 15★.

- Upgrade Hospitals, Barracks, Stable, and tier 3 troops while waiting.
- Upgrade City Hall to 16.

### City Hall 16 to 17

Requirements: Quarry 16★, Tavern 16★, Wall 16★, Farm 16★, Barracks 16★, Hospital 16★.

- Spend speedups on Quarry and Tavern.
- Upgrade City Hall to 17.

## Pacing tips (stage 1 to 17)

- Keep two builders active (VIP or gem).
- Use speedups only on City Hall and ★ buildings.
- Ask alliance help before each long upgrade.
- Gather stone and wood continuously; they are the bottleneck.
- Research Construction and Economy in Academy from the start.

---

## Strategic transition at level 17

Once level 17 is reached, the focus is to increase gathering efficiency as fast as possible.

1. Upgrade only one Farm, one Lumber Mill, one Quarry, and one Goldmine to level 17.
2. Then upgrade training buildings (Infantry Barracks, Archery Range, and Stable) to level 17.
3. Siege Workshop can stay at level 10 for this goal.
4. After that, upgrade Alliance Center, Academy, Tavern, Wall, Storehouse, and Hospital to level 17.

If resource selling is still low or you do not need high production yet, the most efficient route is to push directly to City Hall 22. At that level, you unlock the fifth march and greatly increase gathering capacity without overspending on secondary upgrades.

For farm accounts, City Hall 22 is an excellent balance between investment, time, and return.
