# Farm Account Creation Guide

**[Español](README_es.md) | English | [Português](README_pt.md) | [Tiếng Việt](README_vi.md) | [Bahasa Indonesia](README_id.md) | [Français](README_fr.md)**

Recommended path to push farm accounts from City Hall 1 to 22.

## Goal

- Reach City Hall 22 quickly to unlock the fifth march.
- Prioritize required buildings and avoid unnecessary costs.

## Key phases

1. City Hall 1 to 17: complete minimum required buildings per level.
2. At 17: upgrade only 1 Farm, 1 Lumber Mill, 1 Quarry, and 1 Goldmine to 17.
3. Upgrade key training and support buildings to 17.
4. Continue to City Hall 22 using only mandatory requirements.

## Priorities

- Keep 2 builders active at all times.
- Use speedups on City Hall and required buildings.
- Request alliance help before long upgrades.
- Prioritize stone and wood gathering.

## Expected outcome

- Fifth march unlocked at City Hall 22.
- Better gathering efficiency for farm accounts.
